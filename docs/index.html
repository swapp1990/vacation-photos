About the App

Vacation Photos automatically finds and organizes your travel photos by detecting when you were away from home. No more scrolling through thousands of photos to find that perfect beach sunset!

Frequently Asked Questions

How does it work?

The app uses your photo's location data to identify pictures taken more than 50 miles from your home location, then groups them into trips.

Why do I need to grant location permission?

We need your current location once to determine your "home" area. This is only used locally and never sent to any server.

Some photos are missing from my trips?

Photos without location data (GPS coordinates) cannot be grouped into trips. Some photos taken indoors or with location services disabled may not appear.

How do I delete my data?

Simply delete the app. All data is stored locally on your device and will be removed when you uninstall.
